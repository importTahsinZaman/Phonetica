# Phonetica

Countless families immigrate to the United States every year and struggle to help their child succeed in school which often snowballs into the child having difficulty later in life finding a job and overall feeling that they have potential and are included in American society. While children whose families have been speaking English for generations are able to go home and receive extra help and even just soak up more English due to their environment, immigrant students have to wait until school the next day to hopefully get 45 minutes of help in ELA class. Phonetica hopes to change this by giving immigrant families the ability to sit down with their children and read English story books together, despite not knowing how to perfectly read English themselves. Phonetica is powered by AI OCR and translation sofwtare which allows for easy scanning of text.
